# Research Strategy

Refine the idea only after evidence gates have run.

## Workflow

1. Preserve the user's broad interest where possible.
2. Narrow the contribution to a testable, differentiable hypothesis.
3. Define baselines, datasets, metrics, ablations, failure cases, and reviewer-facing evidence.
4. Write `docs/proposal/revised_idea.md`, `experiment_plan.md`, `first_4_week_plan.md`, and `paper_story.md`.

## Guardrails

- Do not synthesize a better idea before novelty and feasibility checks.
- Avoid vague framework contributions unless paired with measurable claims.
- New claims must identify the evidence needed to support them.
